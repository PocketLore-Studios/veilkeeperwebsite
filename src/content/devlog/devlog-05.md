Combat readability continues to improve as the tactical loop takes shape. Units now visually dim after they’ve acted, making it easier to see which units are still available during a turn.

This also helps clarify enemy turns. With multiple units acting in sequence, the battlefield is easier to follow and the flow of combat is more understandable at a glance.

These changes don’t add new mechanics, but they make the existing systems much clearer. As more interactions are added, readability will become increasingly important.

## Current progress

- Units dim after acting
- Clearer turn readability
- Improved enemy turn flow
- Better battlefield clarity
- More readable multi-unit combat

Next up is continuing to improve combat feedback and adding stronger indicators for targeting and damage resolution.
