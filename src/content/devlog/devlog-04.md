Units can now attack and be defeated on the battlefield. This marks the first complete combat interaction in Veilkeeper’s tactical layer.

With selection and movement already in place, this update adds targeting and damage resolution. Units can now engage enemies, reduce health, and remove defeated units from the board.

This is the first point where the prototype starts to resemble an actual combat loop instead of isolated systems. Movement, positioning, and interaction now work together.

## Current progress

- Unit targeting
- Attack resolution
- Health reduction
- Unit defeat / removal
- First full combat interaction

Next up is improving feedback and readability as the combat loop continues to take shape.
