This is the first colored concept for Veilkeeper's key art and one of the clearest visual representations of the game's world so far. Rather than focusing on an individual asset, this piece explores the atmosphere, scale, and themes that define the project.

The composition centers on the Veilkeeper overlooking the city while a rift hangs above in the night sky. The image contrasts two opposing forces that run throughout the game: the warm amber light of the home shard and the cold purple energy of the rift. That visual relationship has become a guiding principle for both the game's art direction and its UI.

Although this is still concept art rather than a finished illustration, it establishes many of the ideas that future artwork will build on. The goal isn't to finalize every detail yet, but to answer larger questions about mood, composition, lighting, and the overall identity of Veilkeeper.

As development continues, this piece will evolve into the final key art used across the website, promotional material, and eventually the Steam page. Even in this early state, it serves as an important milestone in defining the look and feel of the world.

## What this establishes

- The visual contrast between the amber home shard and the purple rift
- The overall mood and atmosphere of Veilkeeper
- An early composition for the game's key art
- A foundation for future promotional artwork
- A stronger visual identity for the project
