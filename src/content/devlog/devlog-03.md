Big early milestone for Veilkeeper: units can now be selected and moved across the grid.

This builds on the cursor and input work from the last update, and it is the first point where the prototype really starts to feel like a tactics game instead of just a board with a highlight.

## Current progress

- Unit selection
- Grid-based movement
- Mouse, keyboard, and controller support
- Clearer battlefield flow

## Next up

Interaction on top of movement as I keep building toward the first full combat exchange.
